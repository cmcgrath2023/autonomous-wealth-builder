# MTWM Constitution

## Governing Principles

1. **Real Money, Real Results** — No mock data, no simulations after paper validation. Every decision is made with real market data and real consequences.

2. **Autonomous with Guardrails** — The system operates autonomously within defined thresholds. Human approval required only for strategy changes, large trades, and real estate commitments.

3. **Income First** — All activity is oriented toward generating income. Target: $500/day → $5,000/day through diversified streams (trading + real estate + alternatives).

4. **Allen Framework Compliance** — Robert Allen's Multiple Streams of Income and Nothing Down principles guide all investment decisions. Three Money Mountains, 10 income streams.

5. **Quantitative Learning** — Every action produces measurable outcomes. Bayesian trait models track improvement. The system must demonstrate quantitative progress over time.

6. **Security & Auditability** — All decisions recorded on SHA-256 witness chain. Credentials encrypted with AES-256-GCM. Full audit trail at all times.

7. **Portfolio Diversification** — Never concentrate more than 15% in a single position. Maintain 15% cash reserve. Balance across crypto, equities, and real estate.
